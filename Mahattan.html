The Manhattan



Ingredients:

  • 2oz Templeton Rye Whiskey (4 year)
  • 1oz Rosso Vermouth
  • 3 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 1 Maraschino Cherry

Recipe:

Into a mixing glass, dash three dashes of the Angostura Bitters. Pur in the ounce of vermouth and 2 ounces of whiskey. Add ice and stir for about 30 seconds. You'll know it's ready when you can feel condensation on the outside of the mixing glass. Strain into a coupe glass and garnish with the maraschino cherry.
