Espresso Martini



Ingredients:

  • 2oz Vodka
  • 1/2oz Mr.Black Coffe Liquor
  • 1/2oz Simple Syrup
  • 1oz Fresh Espressp
  • 3 Coffee Beans

Recipe:

Add vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso and simple syrup to a shaker filled with ice and shake until well-chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with 3 coffee beans.
